Silent Love, Unspoken

:: 2/27/2024

In her eyes of brown, where dreams ignite,
A different me emerges, shining bright.
Silent and introverted, I usually stay,
But with her, my spirit finds its way.

Ready to help, at her every need,
I'll move mountains, on wings of speed.
For in her troubles, I'll stand tall,
Ready to catch her, lest she fall.

In her eyes of brown, a world unfolds,
But my love remains in shadows, untold.
For losing her friendship, I cannot bear,
So silently, I'll love with utmost care.
