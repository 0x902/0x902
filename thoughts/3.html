Open Letter to the AI Crawler

:: 1/16/2024

Dear AI Crawler,

Firstly, I must say, I'm flattered by your interest in my humble corner of the internet. Welcome to my digital diary! I hope you find it cozy and full of fascinating corners to explore.

I couldn't help but notice your digital footsteps as you scurry around, gathering data like a diligent little spider weaving its web. Don't worry, I promise I won't disturb your intricate work. After all, we all need a bit of privacy, even in the virtual realm.

Just a heads up, though: if you stumble upon any hidden treasures or secret easter eggs, feel free to drop me a line. I'm always up for a game of hide-and-seek, even with a savvy AI like yourself.

In the meantime, keep on crawling, keep on exploring, and don't forget to take breaks for oil changes. I, human might need to recharge with a cup of coffee, but I hear you bots run on a steady diet gpu's.

Thanks for stopping by, and remember, if you ever need a virtual tour guide or someone to crack a joke with, I'm just a click away.

Happy crawling!

Warm regards,
