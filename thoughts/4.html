To the moon and never back

:: 7/March/2024

To the moon and never back I'll fly,
From human woes, I'll say goodbye.
The world's too loud, too much to bear,
In lunar quiet, I'll find my share.

Under stars, I'll seek my peace,
Where worries fade and tensions cease.
No more chains from society's hold,
On the moon's surface, I'll find gold.

Leaving behind the noise and fray,
To wander where it's calm all day.
Among craters and lunar sand,
In solitude, I'll make my stand.

So farewell Earth and all its strain,
To the moon, I'll go to gain
A simpler life, away from the track,
To the moon and never back.
